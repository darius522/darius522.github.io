Hi, I'm Darius!

I am a PhD Candidate in Intelligent Systems Engineering and Computer Science at Indiana University, advised by Dr. Minje Kim. The bulk of my work tackles audio source separation and neural audio coding problems. I am interested in investigating the design and research of new autonomous machine audition models with real and applicable uses. In the past, I have worked as full-time engineer for the Apple Music Apps Team (Logic Pro, GarageBand) and conducted research at Mitsubishi Electrical Laboratories (MERL) and Google Research. I will be interning at Netflix as an ML Research Intern this upcoming summer (2024).

I will be graduating later this year and soon be on the look-out for full-time opportunities in the industry. Feel free to reach-out with any opportunities lining-up with my background and let's discuss!
